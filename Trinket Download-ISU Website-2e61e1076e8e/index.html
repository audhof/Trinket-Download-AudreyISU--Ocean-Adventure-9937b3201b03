About Me

Before taking grade 11 computer science I had never done any coding before, but I had an interest in it. I chose to take this class because I would like to work in data science when I grow up and coding is a necessary skill for that field. Throughout the year I learned a lot about how to code and the different career paths you can go into. I learned how to make drawings in python using turtle, how to do math on python, how to solve word problems in python and how to use html.

Career Paths

Different Career Paths I Would Consider That Involve Commputer Science:

Software Developer: Software developers are responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications that meet specific user needs. I am interested in this job because of the career growth opportunities, and the problem solving and creativity involved.

Data Scientist: A data scientist analyzes data and builds statistical models with insights used to inform business decisions. I am interested in this job because of the high salary and ability to work in many different fields.

Game

My game, Ocean Adventure is an adventure game about a pirate named Captain Blackbeard on the quest for revenge and hidden treasure. It involves finding clues, fighting monsters and answering riddles in order to win the game. There are 6 different interactive rooms with characters to talk to and places to explore and 3 challenges you must pass before moving on. It results in three different endings depending on what you choose to do with the treasure at the end.

Tips To Win:

  1. Investigate every item until you know where to go next
  2. Listen carefully to every clue you receive, it may be helpful later on
  3. What you choose to do at the end will determine your unique ending
  4. You can fail a challenge, but you will have to redo it to move on
